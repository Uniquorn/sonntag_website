Sonntag

Delicious aged and fresh cheeses, made from caaaashew nuts and other plant based ingredients.

Sonntag artisanal foods celebrate age-old European cheesemaking and fermentation techniques - no cows needed. Real food that fits into your sustainable and healthy lifestyle.

We at Sonntag believe that a plant based diet shouldn't require you to renounce familial and hearty foods like beautifully ripened blue cheese. Perfect for your next grazing platter, paired with a wine or as an irresistible gift.

Sonntag is plastic-free and takes on the challenge of zero-waste manufacturing.
